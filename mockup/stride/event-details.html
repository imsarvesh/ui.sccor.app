Event Details

Morning Ride

calendar_today Sat, July 27, 2024
schedule 8:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Join us for a refreshing morning ride through the scenic trails of Redwood Park. This event is perfect for cyclists of all levels, offering a mix of paved roads and gentle off-road paths. We'll start at the main parking lot and follow a loop that showcases the park's natural beauty, including towering redwoods, lush ferns, and occasional wildlife sightings. The ride will be approximately 20 miles, with a few stops for water and rest.
